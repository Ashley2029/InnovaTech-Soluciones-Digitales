Hello teacher and classmates! Today, I want to share with you my favorite tourist destination: Cusco. I chose this city because its history is fascinating. Many countries have their own wonders, but Peru has Machu Picchu, and that pride is ours. I want to show you why you should plan your next vacation there."
---
First, let’s talk about the weather. In Cusco, the weather is very special. Its mornings are bright and sunny, but its nights are freezing!
Because of this, I have some recommendations for your luggage. I always bring my warmest jacket, and you should bring yours too. If you travel with friends, remind them to bring their ponchos because it rains unexpectedly. Don't worry about the rain; the city looks beautiful when its streets are wet."
---
There are many places to explore. For example, the Sacsayhuamán fortress is incredible. Its stones are so big that people wonder how they moved them!
Then, we have the San Pedro Market. It is a favorite spot of mine. You can see the locals selling their handmade crafts. I bought a beautiful sweater there; the style is typical of theirs (the artisans). You can also buy a souvenir for your family, and maybe one for mine too!"
----
You cannot visit Cusco and not eat! Its gastronomy is world-famous. I love 'Lomo Saltado', but maybe 'Cuy' is a favorite of yours.
My main suggestion is: drink coca tea. It helps with altitude sickness. I have my own bottle of water everywhere, and you should carry yours to stay hydrated while you walk through its ancient streets."
